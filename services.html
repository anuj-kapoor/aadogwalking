In-Home Dog Sitting

Our Dog Sitting Service is tailored to your pup, as all dogs have different needs. We will come and care for your dog in your home. We arrange a convenient time to come and see you and your pet for a free consultation before you go away for the first time. This involves filling out a care plan which covers everything we need to know about your pet, therefore giving "you and your pet" the best possible service. This is a great service for any pet owner who doesn’t like to leave their dog. For just "$55.00 per night" we will come and care for your dog in your own home. This is great for anyone wanting to go out to an event or even a day’s shopping. We can give you that peace of mind that your pet is being looked after in your own home and of course getting lots of cuddles and TLC. This service allows your dog to be in their familiar environment and follow their normal routines as much as possible. This service is especially great for nervous dogs who don’t like being left alone. We can even send you pictures to your phone or email address whlie Dog Sitting to give you that extra peace of mind.

Custom Pet Care Solutions

Whatever you and your pets need, we can probably do it, and we’ll do it with loving care! When you can’t do something that needs to be done, we’d love to help!

We have lots of successful experience with:

  • Taking your pet to the vet or groomer, as well as any additional pet care destinations.
  • Picking up your pet’s meds, food, kitty litter, best friend, whatever…
  • Staying with your pet at your home while the mobile vet visits and helping the vet while they are there.
  • Adjusting our routines to accommodate blended pet families, short-term critical care needs, end-of-life and hospice needs, etc...
  • Managing home emergencies, such as water leaks, with your approved service providers

We love to help and we love a challenge, so don’t hesitate to ask us if something you and/or your pet need is not on this list!
